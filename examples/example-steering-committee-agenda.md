# Example AI Steering Committee Meeting Agenda

AI steering committees provide oversight for major AI initiatives and help ensure alignment with organizational strategy, risk management, and operational priorities.

---

## Meeting Information

Meeting Type: AI Program Steering Committee  
Frequency: Monthly  
Meeting Duration: 60 minutes

---

## Sample Agenda

### 1. Program Overview (10 minutes)

Program Lead provides a brief summary of overall program status.

Topics may include:

- current program status
- key achievements since last meeting
- overall delivery progress

---

### 2. Initiative Updates (15 minutes)

Review progress on active AI initiatives.

Topics may include:

- pilot progress
- operational integration updates
- model performance improvements

---

### 3. Risk and Compliance Review (10 minutes)

Review risks identified in the program risk register.

Topics may include:

- regulatory considerations
- data privacy concerns
- operational risks

---

### 4. Decisions Required (15 minutes)

Discuss and approve key program decisions.

Examples:

- approval of new AI initiatives
- expansion of pilot programs
- investment in additional data infrastructure

---

### 5. Upcoming Milestones (5 minutes)

Review major upcoming milestones and timelines.

Examples:

- pilot completion
- production deployment
- operational training

---

### 6. Open Discussion (5 minutes)

Opportunity for steering committee members to raise additional questions or considerations.
