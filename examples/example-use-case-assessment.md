# Example AI Use Case Assessment

This example demonstrates how an AI opportunity might be evaluated using the intake and prioritization framework described in this playbook.

---

## Use Case Title

Automated Customer Support Ticket Classification

---

## Business Problem

Customer support teams spend significant time manually reviewing incoming tickets to determine which department should handle the request.

This process slows response times and increases operational workload.

---

## Current Process

1. Support staff manually read incoming tickets.
2. Tickets are categorized and routed to the appropriate team.
3. Misclassification can lead to delays in resolution.

---

## Proposed AI Solution

Use natural language processing (NLP) to automatically classify incoming support tickets based on text content and historical ticket data.

The AI model would recommend the appropriate department for each ticket.

---

## Expected Business Value

- Faster ticket routing
- Reduced manual classification work
- Improved response times
- Higher customer satisfaction

---

## Data Requirements

Potential data sources include:

- historical support ticket data
- ticket categories
- resolution outcomes

Data quality and labeling consistency should be evaluated before model training.

---

## Implementation Considerations

Technical Complexity: Medium

Operational Readiness: Medium

Integration may be required with the customer support platform.

---

## Success Metrics

Success may be measured by:

- classification accuracy
- reduction in manual ticket triage time
- faster ticket resolution times
- improved customer satisfaction scores
