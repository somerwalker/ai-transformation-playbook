# Example 90-Day AI Transformation Plan

This example roadmap illustrates how organizations can launch an AI transformation initiative over the first ninety days while establishing governance, prioritization, and delivery structure.

Organizations beginning an AI transformation often benefit from a structured launch plan.

## Days 1–30: Establish Foundations

- identify key stakeholders
- define governance structure
- establish use case intake process
- gather and document AI opportunities
- assess data availability and readiness

## Days 31–60: Prioritize and Plan

- evaluate use cases using prioritization framework
- identify pilot initiatives
- define delivery teams and responsibilities
- establish reporting cadence
- begin pilot design

## Days 61–90: Launch Pilot Initiatives

- begin pilot development
- monitor technical and operational risks
- establish success metrics
- collect early performance data
- refine roadmap for additional initiatives

This phased approach helps organizations build momentum while maintaining governance and operational discipline.
