# AI Risk and Readiness Assessment

This assessment framework helps organizations identify risks and evaluate whether the necessary data, processes, and operational support exist to successfully implement AI initiatives.

AI initiatives introduce both technical and organizational risks. Evaluating readiness early helps organizations avoid costly failures later in the implementation lifecycle.

## Common Risk Areas

### Data Quality
Incomplete or inconsistent data may undermine model performance.

### Process Ownership
AI initiatives require clear operational owners responsible for outcomes.

### Compliance and Privacy
Sensitive data may introduce legal or regulatory considerations.

### Integration Complexity
AI solutions often need to integrate with existing systems and workflows.

### User Adoption
Even technically successful solutions may fail if users do not trust or adopt them.

## Readiness Indicators

Organizations are more likely to succeed when they have:

- clearly defined business problems
- accessible and reliable data sources
- operational leaders responsible for adoption
- governance mechanisms for risk management

Assessing readiness early helps organizations prioritize initiatives that are most likely to succeed.
