# AI Delivery Operating Model

Successful AI initiatives require coordination across multiple teams, including engineering, data science, operations, and business leadership.

An operating model helps align these groups around shared goals and delivery timelines.

## Key Stages of Delivery

1. **Intake and Evaluation**
   Capture and assess AI opportunities.

2. **Prioritization**
   Select initiatives based on value and readiness.

3. **Pilot Development**
   Develop and validate early versions of the solution.

4. **Operational Deployment**
   Integrate the solution into production workflows.

5. **Measurement and Optimization**
   Track outcomes and refine the solution over time.

## Cross-Team Coordination

Effective delivery requires:

- clear ownership of each stage
- consistent communication between teams
- defined escalation paths for risks and blockers
- regular progress reporting

A structured delivery model ensures AI initiatives move forward with clarity and accountability.
