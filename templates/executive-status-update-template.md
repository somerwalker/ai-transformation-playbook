# AI Program Executive Status Update Template

Use this template to provide leadership with a clear summary of progress, risks, and key decisions for AI initiatives.

Executive updates should focus on business outcomes, major milestones, risks, and decisions requiring leadership attention.

---

## Program Overview

**Program Name**

**Reporting Period**

**Program Lead**

**Executive Sponsor**

---

## Overall Status

Select the status that best reflects the program’s current condition.

- Green – On track
- Yellow – At risk
- Red – Significant issues requiring intervention

Provide a brief explanation of the status.

---

## Key Achievements

Highlight major accomplishments during the reporting period.

Examples:

- completion of pilot model development
- successful integration with operational systems
- improved model accuracy during testing
- user training and onboarding completed

---

## Current Work in Progress

Summarize major initiatives currently underway.

Examples:

- pilot deployment to selected business units
- data pipeline development
- operational workflow integration
- monitoring and performance evaluation

---

## Risks and Issues

Identify risks that could impact delivery or outcomes.

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation Strategy | Owner |
|-----|-------|--------------------|------|
| Example: Data quality issues | High | Implement data validation procedures | Data Lead |

---

## Key Decisions Required

List decisions requiring leadership input.

| Decision | Context | Recommended Action | Decision Owner |
|---------|--------|-------------------|---------------|
| Example: Approve expansion of pilot program | Pilot results show positive outcomes | Expand pilot to additional teams | Executive Sponsor |

---

## Upcoming Milestones

Outline major upcoming program milestones.

Examples:

- completion of pilot evaluation
- model deployment to production
- operational training rollout
- performance review and optimization

---

## Metrics and Outcomes

Provide measurable indicators of program progress.

Examples:

- reduction in manual processing time
- increase in prediction accuracy
- user adoption rate
- cost savings or efficiency gains

---

## Summary

Provide a concise summary of the program’s overall direction, key progress, and any support needed from leadership.
