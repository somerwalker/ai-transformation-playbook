# AI Program Decision Log Template

Use this template to record significant decisions made during the planning, development, and deployment of AI initiatives.

A decision log helps maintain transparency, track the rationale behind key choices, and ensure alignment across teams and leadership.

## Decision Categories

Major decisions within AI initiatives often fall into several categories.

### Technical Decisions
Architecture, infrastructure platforms, model design, and system integrations.

### Data Decisions
Selection of data sources, data governance policies, and data quality standards.

### Governance Decisions
Approval processes, oversight mechanisms, and accountability structures.

### Operational Decisions
Deployment processes, monitoring practices, and operational ownership.

---

## Best Practices for Maintaining a Decision Log

- Document decisions as they occur rather than retroactively.
- Clearly record the rationale for each decision.
- Identify stakeholders involved in the decision-making process.
- Review the log regularly during program governance meetings.
- Maintain a clear status for each decision.

A well-maintained decision log helps organizations avoid confusion, support accountability, and preserve institutional knowledge throughout the lifecycle of an AI initiative.
