# AI Stakeholder Mapping Template

Use this template to identify and align stakeholders involved in an AI initiative.

AI transformations typically involve multiple teams across business, technical, and governance functions. A clear stakeholder map helps ensure that responsibilities, expectations, and communication channels are well defined.

---

## Stakeholder Overview

| Stakeholder | Role | Department | Responsibility | Influence Level | Engagement Strategy |
|-------------|------|------------|---------------|----------------|--------------------|
| Executive Sponsor | Strategic oversight | Leadership | Ensures initiative aligns with organizational priorities | High | Regular executive updates |
| Business Owner | Operational ownership | Business Unit | Responsible for outcomes and adoption | High | Weekly coordination meetings |
| Program Lead | Delivery coordination | PMO / Transformation | Oversees initiative progress and reporting | High | Cross-team coordination |
| Technical Lead | Technical implementation | Engineering / Data Science | Responsible for architecture and development | Medium | Technical planning sessions |
| Compliance Advisor | Regulatory oversight | Legal / Compliance | Reviews risk and regulatory considerations | Medium | Compliance reviews |
| End Users | Solution users | Operations / Business Teams | Provide feedback and adopt AI solution | Medium | Training and user feedback sessions |

---

## Stakeholder Influence Matrix

Stakeholders can be categorized based on their level of influence and interest.

| Category | Description | Engagement Approach |
|----------|-------------|--------------------|
| High Influence / High Interest | Key decision-makers and program leaders | Frequent updates and involvement in decision making |
| High Influence / Low Interest | Senior leaders who require periodic updates | Executive briefings |
| Low Influence / High Interest | Operational stakeholders and users | Regular communication and feedback loops |
| Low Influence / Low Interest | Peripheral stakeholders | Informational updates as needed |

---

## Communication Plan

Define how and when stakeholders will receive updates.

| Communication Type | Audience | Frequency | Format |
|--------------------|----------|----------|--------|
| Executive Status Update | Executive Sponsor / Steering Committee | Monthly | Executive summary report |
| Program Review | Program Team | Weekly | Delivery status meeting |
| Technical Review | Engineering / Data Teams | Bi-weekly | Technical planning session |
| User Feedback | End Users | During pilot phases | Workshops or surveys |

---

## Notes

Stakeholder alignment is critical for successful AI initiatives. Regular communication and clearly defined responsibilities help ensure that initiatives maintain momentum and deliver meaningful outcomes.
