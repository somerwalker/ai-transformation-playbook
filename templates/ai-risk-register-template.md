# AI Risk Register Template

Use this template to identify, document, and monitor risks associated with AI initiatives throughout the delivery lifecycle.

Maintaining a risk register helps organizations track potential issues, assign ownership, and implement mitigation strategies before risks impact operations.

## Risk Categories

Risks associated with AI initiatives often fall into several categories:

### Data Risks
Issues related to data availability, quality, bias, or access.

Examples:
- incomplete datasets
- inconsistent data sources
- biased training data

### Technical Risks
Challenges associated with system architecture, model performance, or integration.

Examples:
- model performance degradation
- integration failures
- system scalability limitations

### Compliance and Regulatory Risks
Legal or regulatory constraints associated with data usage and AI decision-making.

Examples:
- privacy regulations
- financial or healthcare compliance requirements
- data residency constraints

### Operational Risks
Challenges related to adoption, process integration, or organizational readiness.

Examples:
- unclear process ownership
- insufficient training for users
- resistance to adoption

---

## Risk Management Process

AI program teams should regularly review the risk register to:

- identify emerging risks
- update mitigation strategies
- assign clear ownership
- communicate risks to leadership

Risk reviews are typically conducted during governance meetings or program status reviews.
